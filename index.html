About Me

Hannah is a 20 year old singer-songwriter from Orange County who is frequently compared to Colbie Caillat, Taylor Swift, and Norah Jones. Hannah has been performing for years at venues across Southern California, Dallas, Nashville, and Austin to include the Bluebird Cafe, Lido Marina Village, The House of Blues, Deep Ellum, The Curtain Club, Opening Bell Coffee, The Grapevine Opry, Central Market, and coffee shops across Southern California. She was on the main stage at the Austin Pecan Street Festival and was a performer at the 30A Songwriter's Festival.

Hannah's music is played frequently on KNTU and local radio stations, and her music video for "Empty Box" was recently selected by White Knight World Media/Pulse Records to be featered at over 10,000 venues across America. Hannah's song, "Lucky You" was also a finalist in the International Songwriting Competition.
