About Me

Hannah is a 20 year old singer-songwriter from Orange County who is frequently compared to Colbie Caillat, Taylor Swift, and Norah Jones. Hannah has been performing for years at venues across Southern California, Dallas, Nashville, and Austin.

This includes the Bluebird Cafe, Lido Marina Village. The House of Blues, Deep Ellum, The Curtain Club, Opening Bell Coffee, The Grapevine Opry, Central Market, and coffee shops across Southern California. She was on the main stage at the Austin Pecan Street Festival and was a performer at the 30A Songwriter's Festival.
